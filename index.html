What is the heat transfer?
The heat transfer is a describtion of the kinetics of energy storage, transport and energy transformation by principal energy carries phonons, electrons, fluid particles and photons.
How to calculate heat transfer?
To calculate heat transfer you should use the following formula:
Q = m * c * ΔT
where:
Q = heat transferred
m = mass
c = specific heat
ΔT = difference in temperature
Why use heat transfer?
The heat transfer is used in order to preserve, increase or decrease the temperature in a wide variety of circumstances.
